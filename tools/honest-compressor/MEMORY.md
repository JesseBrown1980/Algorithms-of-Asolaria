  plus pair selector. Omnispindle lesson dfc4e209-edcc-4bb3-8b33-ce85023fbe56

- `dfc4e209-edcc-4bb3-8b33-ce85023fbe56` — operator correction defining
  `PRIME_RULE_OF_THREE_DUALITY`: `N=3m`, select two members of every
  prime-addressed triad, evaluate both polarities, and parity-derive or count
  the omitted member as a residual. This also corrects the overly restrictive
  intermediate interpretation `N=3p`; the prime indexes the triad rather than
  requiring `m` itself to be prime.

- Operator correction (2026-07-19): a worse measured score for one mapping is
  `MEASURED_ACER_NEGATIVE_AT_THIS_PARAMETERIZATION`, not a failed or rejected
  lane. Ask the Fabric before assigning a lane disposition. A delivery ACK or
  deterministic keyword/domain convergence receipt is not a substantive
  owner/HELM ruling; absent such a ruling, keep the lane open. Safety HOLDs
  (for example, an unsafe monolithic allocation) remain valid and must state
  the concrete resource boundary rather than being generalized to the idea.

- Operator correction (2026-07-19): **warm up the system step by step**. A
  warm-up order is a sequence of bounded ignition gates, never permission to
  start every daemon, spindle, worker, GPU batch, or corpus gulp at once. Ask
  the live Fabric for the specific next lane; start one smallest unit; verify
  its process, route, receipt, CPU/GPU/RAM, and rollback boundary; then hold
  scale-up until that receipt is green. Holds are scoped to their named lane
  and must not be projected onto an independently measured sister-organ lane.
